ABOUTUS

Vision

To develop an “Aatmanirbhar (Self-Dependent) Society.”

Mission

The NSS is working with a mission to solve problems of community and society by using knowledge, skills, technology and people to make a self-reliant and self-sufficient society.

Objectives

  1. Identify the needs and problems of the community and involve them in problem-solving
  2. Develop among themselves a sense of social and civic responsibility.
  3. Utilize their knowledge in finding practical solutions to individual and community problems.
  4. Develop competence required for group-living and sharing of responsibilities.
  5. Gain skills in mobilizing community participation.
  6. Acquire leadership qualities and democratic attitudes.
  7. Develop capacity to meet emergencies and natural disasters.

Preamble

  1. Every member of this club can be a member of maximum any other two clubs.
  2. NSS Club will follow the Calendar Year.
  3. Membership of NSS Club- Free
  4. Membership is open to any currently enrolled Students.
  5. This Club shall not discriminate on the basis of race, colour, ethnicity, national origin, age, gender, disability or physical disability, religion, gender orientation, department, procedures, and practices. This policy will include but is not limited to recruiting, membership, organization activities.
  6. Only currently enrolled students are allowed to be a member and run for club .
  7. For certain activities (such as Village Surveys, Visiting the Orphanage, Visiting Old Age Home, etc.) the club , after consultation with the Management, may solicit the help .
