Plantation Drive

Title: Green Earth Plantation Drive

Description: The Green Earth Plantation Drive, an initiative aimed at making a positive impact on our environment and fostering a sense of responsibility towards nature. This community-driven event invites individuals of all ages to come together for a day of tree planting and environmental awareness.

Health Care Drive

Title: Community Health Camp

Description: Our Community Health Camp, an initiative dedicated to providing essential healthcare services, promoting health awareness, and fostering a healthier community. This event is designed to bring together healthcare professionals, volunteers, and community members for a day of health-focused activities and services.
